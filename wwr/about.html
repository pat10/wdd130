White Water Rafting Co.

Welcome to White Water Rafting Co.! We are thrilled to have you here and excited to share our passion for adventure and the great outdoors with you. Our company was founded in 2005 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to create unforgettable experiences on the water.

History

Our rafting business began when the first caveman decided to float the river on a log. Starting with our founder, Thag Simmons, our business has been passed from generation to generation, and we hope to continue until the heat death of the universe.

Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a well-known rafting company that attracts adventurers from all over the world. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to providing safe and thrilling rafting trips that showcase the beauty of nature.
